1. Introduction

Island Roamers ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information you provide through our lead generation features on Meta platforms (Facebook and Instagram).

2. Information We Collect

When you submit a lead form through our Meta advertisements or organic lead generation features, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Any additional information you choose to provide

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry about our services
  • Provide you with the information or service you requested
  • Send you relevant information related to your inquiry
  • Fulfill any offer terms promoted in our lead generation features

We Will Never Sell Your Data

Your information will never be sold to third parties under any circumstances.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We may share your information with trusted service providers who help us operate our business, but only to the extent necessary to fulfill the service you requested. Any third party receiving your data:

  • Must maintain appropriate security measures

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against:

  • Unauthorized access
  • Unlawful processing
  • Accidental loss, destruction, or damage

Our security measures are proportionate to the sensitivity of the information and the risks involved.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Object to processing of your data

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by applicable law.

8. Minors

Our lead generation features are not targeted to minors. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 18.

9. Compliance with Meta Lead Ad Terms

Our use of lead generation features complies with Meta's Lead Ad Terms, including:

  • Obtaining all necessary permissions before using your data
  • Not collecting sensitive or prohibited information
  • Providing clear disclosures about how we'll use your information
  • Honoring all offer terms and consents you agree to
  • Maintaining appropriate data security measures
